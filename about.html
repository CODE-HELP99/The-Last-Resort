The Last Resort: A Timeless Culinary Legacy

Owner: Antonio Lorenzo

Founded: 1785

The Last Resort has a rich history that dates back to 1785 when it was established by the esteemed chef and restaurateur Antonio Lorenzo. Antonio, a name synonymous with culinary excellence in 18th-century Italy, brought his passion for gourmet food and refined dining to life with this iconic establishment.

Origins and Vision:

Antonio Lorenzo, a visionary in the art of continental cuisine, sought to create a haven where travelers and locals alike could experience the finest in European flavors. Inspired by his extensive travels across Europe and his admiration for classical Italian dishes, Lorenzo envisioned a restaurant that would serve as both a culinary destination and a sanctuary for the discerning palate.

The Restaurant:

Nestled in the heart of Florence, The Last Resort quickly gained acclaim for its elegant ambiance and sophisticated menu. The restaurant's name, "The Last Resort," reflected its role as the ultimate destination for those seeking a memorable dining experience after a long journey. Lorenzo's dedication to quality was evident in every aspect of the restaurant, from its meticulously crafted dishes to its refined service.
